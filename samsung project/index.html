      Samsung Account

      1. What is Samsung Account?
      Samsung Account is a free integrated membership service that enables you to use Samsung service on smartphones, tablets, websites, TVs and other devices. Enjoy various Samsung service through your Samsung account, without having to sign up for each service separately.
      2. How do I sign in/out?
      You can sign in by clicking on the 'Log in / Sign up' link, which appears when you hover over the person icon at the top right of the page.
      3. Why is Samsung Account 2 Step Verification?
      Two-step verification is an enhanced security feature that prevents someone from signing into your Samsung Account. It strengthens the security by using two different authentication factors to verify your credentials: password, and an extra security code. When signing into your account, you will enter your password and shortly after receive a verification code to your mobile phone via text message. If someone attempts to sign into your account with your password, they will not be able to access your account due to not receiving the additional verification code.
